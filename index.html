Envision3D: Efficient Dense Views Generation
with Anchor Views Interpolation for Image to 3D





We show on the left side that Envision3D generates 32 dense, multi-view consistent images and extracts high-quality 3D content from one input image. On the top right side, we present that incorporating fewer views leads to low-quality 3D content. On the bottom right side, we highlight the superior consistency of our method across different views compared to the video diffusion model-based method.
